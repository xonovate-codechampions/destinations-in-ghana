Top Destinations in Ghana

Explore breathtaking places that tell the story of Ghana's beauty and history.

Cape Coast Castle

A UNESCO World Heritage site and a key location in the history of the transatlantic slave trade.

Kakum National Park

Walk the famous canopy walkway and experience the rainforest from above.

Lake Volta

One of the largest man-made lakes in the world, perfect for boat cruises and scenic views.

Mole National Park

Ghana’s largest wildlife park — see elephants, antelopes, and more in their natural habitat.
